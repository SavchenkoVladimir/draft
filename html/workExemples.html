Repeat the words.

This is the trainer for english words repetition. It is a single page where you can repeat 15 random words in an iteration. During the training you are presented one english word against true and wrong russian words. You have only 5 seconds to reply. Here are implemented AJAX query, transporting data in JSON, random number generation.
